Key Takeaways
====================
- HPC improved total runtime for all models, with total speed-ups ranging from 3.282× to 9.120×.
- The strongest overall speed-up was observed for Basic (9.120×).
- The smallest overall speed-up was observed for Hybrid (3.282×).
- Indexing and generation dominate the overall runtime in both environments.
- The average total speed-up across all models was 5.050×.
- Retrieval does not improve uniformly across all models:
- Basic: 2.454× speed-up in retrieval
- VectorDB: 2.708× slowdown in retrieval
- VectorDB Tuned: 1.604× slowdown in retrieval
- Reranker: 2.412× speed-up in retrieval
- Hybrid: 3.374× slowdown in retrieval
- Hybrid + Rerank: 3.819× speed-up in retrieval
